Welcome

The Kazakh Centre in London is a volunteer-led community for Kazakh residents, students, professionals and friends living in London and the wider UK. We focus on cultural exchange, language support, and building professional and social networks that help members thrive while staying connected to Kazakh traditions.

About Us

The Kazakh Centre in London was founded to provide a welcoming space where Kazakh culture, language and contemporary life intersect. We welcome members from all backgrounds — students, researchers, families, and professionals — who want to celebrate Kazakh identity, meet new people and access practical support while in the UK.

Join or Volunteer

Membership is open and free. Volunteers are always welcome to help run events, lead language sessions, or support communications. To join or volunteer, please contact us via the form below.
